About

Laura is a Colombian native, holds a Bachelor's degree in Physics, conferred in 2020 by the National Pedagogical University of Colombia, where she excelled as a scholarship recipient. Following her graduation, she secured a second scholarship at the Military University Nueva Granada in 2021, contributing to an artificial intelligence-driven assistive robot project. Facing the financial strain and with the scholarship not materializing, Laura made a life-changing decision and moved to the United States, specifically Utah, in 2022. There, she encountered her current husband, who is Japanese. Laura's journey continued as she became a student at Brigham Young University-Idaho, enrolled in the Applied Technology program. In 2023, Laura relocated to Japan, where she currently resides, embracing new opportunities and a promising future. Her diverse experiences and determination have shaped her into a resilient individual pursuing academic and personal growth.
